Who Is Goad?

Goad is of Mariana Trench origin, from challenger deep to be precise. Don't let his seemingly shallow water fish appearance fool you, he can take the form of many, even sheep. Goad moved from the Mariana to Alaska at an age of 8,340. He stayed in Alaska for 0.02 seconds as a pit-stop to get some food and supplies before heading to The Bermuda Triangle. This is where he set up his underwater kingdom. He is living in the Bermuda Triangle to this day, and is 8,379 years old.


Goad's Abilities:

Goad aswell as being immortal and unkillable, he also posses other otherworldly powers. Such as; telepathy, telekinesis, destructive powers, infinate money glitch, reality bending powers, teleportotion, high speeds, time traveling, and the ability to summon followers to his side.
